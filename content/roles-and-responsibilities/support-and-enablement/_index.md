## üìû Help Desk Technician (Tier 1/2)

| | Simple Outline | Extended Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Role** | Provides **first-line technical support** and troubleshooting for internal employees or external customers. | The **front-line service role** responsible for immediate triage, resolution of basic issues, and logging of complex problems for escalation. |
| **Key Responsibilities** | **Password resets**, ticket logging, basic software/hardware troubleshooting, documentation, **user setup/onboarding**. | * **Incident Triage:** Quickly assessing the severity and category of incoming issues (phone, email, chat). * **Remote Support:** Using remote tools to troubleshoot local PC, network, or application issues. * **Knowledge Base Management:** Creating and updating articles so users can self-serve solutions. * **Escalation:** Handing off unresolved or complex issues to specialized teams (Tier 2/3, Networking, or App Development). |
| **Scope & Focus** | End-user systems (laptops, phones, local networks) and basic application access. | Focuses on **customer service** and **rapid initial resolution** (First Call Resolution - FCR). Manages the interaction between the user and the central IT services. |
| **Key Metrics** | **First Call Resolution (FCR) rate**, average **ticket resolution time**, **user satisfaction score**. | |

## ü§ù Customer Success Engineer (CSE)

| | Simple Outline | Extended Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Role** | Provides **technical guidance** and support to customers post-sale, focused on maximizing product adoption and value. | A hybrid role that combines technical product knowledge with business acumen, ensuring customers successfully implement and utilize the software to achieve their goals. |
| **Key Responsibilities** | **Onboarding technical clients**, integration assistance, **product training**, troubleshooting **complex deployments**. | * **Health Checks:** Proactively reviewing customer usage data to identify adoption challenges or potential churn risk. * **Technical Q&A:** Answering deep technical questions about API usage, system configuration, and best practices. * **Feedback Loop:** Acting as a conduit between the customer and the Product/Engineering teams to relay feature requests and usability issues. |
| **Scope & Focus** | Customer relationship health, product usage, and driving feature adoption. | Focuses on **retention and expansion**. They are key to ensuring the customer receives a high Return on Investment (ROI) from the product. |
| **Key Metrics** | **Customer retention rate**, **product adoption score**, average **time-to-value**. | |
